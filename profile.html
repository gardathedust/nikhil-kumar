Important Values / Learnings

  • Continuous learning — keeping myself updated by listening to relevant podcasts, reading books, taking paid courses, or learning new skills
  • Pushing my team, peers, and external partners to improve continuously

  • Organizational culture is very important
  • I avoid staying in a comfort zone for too long
  • Big-bang re-architecture and redesign projects are often counterproductive — they tend to be top-down driven, aimed at showcasing technical initiative rather than delivering measurable business impact
